# Cap Experiment — CGR-1 Daily Record
Cap is a 90-day currency experiment: a unit of account defined by a public gold reference, without exchange or circulation.
To interpret: “1 Cap” is defined by the daily CGR-1 number recorded below for that date.

## Fixed Rule
“The Cap Gold Reference (CGR-1) shall equal the publicly reported London gold reference (USD per troy ounce), published on a delayed basis, rounded to two decimal places.”

This rule is fixed for the duration of the experiment.

## Duration
This experiment runs for 90 consecutive days.
No extensions are planned.
